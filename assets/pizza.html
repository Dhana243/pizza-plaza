Fresh & full of flavour from our family to yours.

At Slowdough Pizzas we're a family run business bringing a fresh, whole-foods approach to the traditional treats you know and love. Our handcrafted sourdough pizzas and artisan ice-creams are made in small batches with real ingredients, and no added nasties. With house made vegan cheese and gluten free bases available too, we've got flavoursome options the whole family can enjoy.
